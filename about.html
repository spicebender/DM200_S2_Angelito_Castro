Who Even Am I?

Storyboard Supervisor | Visual Development Artist | Creative Designer

I’m Ethan Becker and I’ve been in animation for a hot minute — leading teams and collaborating with creators to tell stronger stories through bold visual storytelling. I grew up in southeast Texas and studied at the Concept Design Academy in Pasadena. Princess Mononoke and Halo sparked my love for storytelling and world-building.

I’ve worked with studios like Netflix, Warner Bros., and DreamWorks, with credits on Pantheon, DOTA: Dragon’s Blood, Invincible, Voltron, Batman: The Long Halloween, and Trese. Outside the studio, I teach through ProjectCity.tv and share what I’ve learned on YouTube and Discord.

Why Work With Me?

I don’t just draw pretty pictures — I build stories that move people. Here’s what I bring to the table:

Visual Development

Characters, worlds, and moments with impact.

Storyboarding

Dynamic, cinematic boards built for emotion and clarity.

Character Design

Effective, meaningful designs that support narrative.
